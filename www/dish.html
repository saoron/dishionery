    Stuffed eggplant in olive oil

    Ingredients Eggplants, tomato puree, garlic, onions, parsley, olive oil

    Half eggplants are braised, fried or grilled and stuffed with sautéed tomato puree, garlic and onions. The dish is then baked in the oven with plenty of olive oil. It is a dish that appears in Turkish cuisine and its name means "the iman swooned" or "fainted" with a variety of explanations for the name, ranging from the iman having fainted over the cost of the olive oil in the dish to fainting from pleasure.

    Region: Throughout Greece

    The dish that came to Greece from Asia Minor with the 1922 Greek refugees after a population exchange between Turkey and Greece, which brought Greeks from Anatolia into Greece.
